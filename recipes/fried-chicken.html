Fried Chicken

Description

This s the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It comibens all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Comibne the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a zip-top bag.
  2. Seal the bag and shake until well-combined.
  3. Pour buttermilk into a shallow bolwl, then dip the chiken untli it's completely soaked in buttermilk.
  4. Place the soaked chicken in the bag with flour mixture, seal, and shake well to coat.
  5. Transfer the coated chicken to a baking sheet, and cover.
  6. Allow the flour coating to become a paste-like consistency.
  7. Pour the oil in a skillet and bring it to high heat.
  8. Brown the chicken on both sides, then reduce the heat, cover the skillet and cook for about 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the cover, return the heat to high, and fry until extra crispy.
  10. Drain the chicken on paper towels.
